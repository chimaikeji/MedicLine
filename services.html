Services

To provide a comprehensive health assessment and education module managed by our qualified nurses and paramedical personnel who will deliver a holistic health care aimed at the total well being of our people and eliminating health complications. This includes:

Blood Pressure monitoring

Heart Rate (Pulse)

Respiration rate

Level of blood in the body (Haemoglobin).

Blood Sugar Level (BSL).

Blood Cholesterol Level

Height, weight and Body Mass Index (BMI).

Temperature

Provision of a 24/7 hours First-Aid Services over the phone to our clients, essentially addressing the health challenges and helping gain insight on their health conditions.

Provision of virtual health (telehealth) programme that will include rapid access to health assessment, care coordination, remote monitoring, patient literacy that will achieve a drastic reduction in the public specialist hospital referrals and visitation through the creation of satellite clinics across the nation.

Clinical Factsheet on all medical conditions provided as required and on request

PROPOSED SERVICES TO IMO STATE GOVERNMENT

The purpose of our services is to help create satellite clinics in all the 27 Local Government Areas of the State by providing:

  • Comprehensive health assessment and education managed by our qualified nurses to provide a holistic care which aim at promoting the general wellbeing of our people thereby preventing health complications.
  • Provision of emergent first aid measures in a 24hrs period over the phone to the entire Imo indigenes addressing any of their health challenges and help them gain insight of their health conditions.
  • Provision of a 24/7 helpline to the entire indigenes and occupants of the state where majority of their health concerns are answered over the phone.
  • Provision of virtual health (telehealth) opportunities including a range of rapid access comprehensive health assessment, care coordination, remote monitoring, and patient literacy services which will see a reduction in the public specialist hospital visitation with the creation of satellite clinic across the entire state as mentioned above.

We hope that for the future and with the help of Imo State government we will be able to deliver alternative models of healthcare across the state which will aim to reduce the burden on our Federal Medical Centers and General Hospitals, allowing them to safely manage patient care.
