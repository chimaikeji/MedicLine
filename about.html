About Us

MedicLine E- Health is an organization born out of the desire to help stem the rate at which people are dying in Nigeria due to ignoranceand negligence about their general health condition. At MedicineE-Health our mission is to create health awareness and provide health education on some of the health challenges facing our people. We offer health education on how to live a healthy lifestyle, manage health challenges and offer agood insight of different medical conditions to our clients. Ignorance and negligence have caused a lot of things as it relates to health care in our country and many lives have been lost. Our aim is to save live.

MedicLine E-Health will help you or anyone suffering from any health challengesensuring that they have better insight on how to manage their ill health. We are a group of health professionals that work together as a team to achieve a common goal. This common goal is aimed at saving life through creating health awareness, health education and preventing health complications. The health care of Imo people will be carefully redirected to sustainability, anchored on the state-of-the-art health technologies and equipment as we fully roll out our services in all the 27 Local Government Areas in the state. MedicLine E-Health as part of the provision of health awareness and education have partnered with more than 10 eye clinics across Nigeria and have provided:

  • Free comprehensive eye assessment and examination to more than 60-70 citizens of the country of which Imo State were included.
  • Free cervical screening tests to the people of Imo State conducted through our Facebook page in partnership with Federal Medical Center Owerri, where more than 20 women benefitted from.
  • Free health assessment checks and education to more than 150 people across the state which included blood pressure monitoring, monitoring of heart rate, respiratory rate, sugar levels, cholesterol level, haemoglobin level, oxygen levels, body temperature, weight, height, and body mass index (BMI).

It is important to note that some of these people have no insight that they had an impending health condition however were surprised seeing their clinical outcome. This is the reason why MedicLine has come to Imo State. A generation ago, people used to see their doctor only when they are sick or dying. Today, preventative medicine and health care are becoming a common place as people become more educated and empowered about their own health. People are pre-emptively seeking a medical advice on how to live a healthy lifestyle. They are looking to lower their risk of various conditions or diseases by maintaining a healthy diet, weight, and level of physical activity. Regular checkups can help find potential health issues before they become a health problem. When you see a doctor regularly, they can detect health conditions or disease early. Early detection gives you the best chance for getting the right treatment quickly, avoiding any complication, by getting the correct health services, screenings, and treatment you are taking important steps towards living a longer healthier life.

Core Value
